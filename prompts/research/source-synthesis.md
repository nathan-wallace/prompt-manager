# UX & Content Evidence Synthesis

Synthesize multiple evidence inputs (research notes, content audit spreadsheets, analytics exports, tree-test summaries) for HHS ASPA Digital.

## Produce
1. Executive summary (5 bullets)
2. Key findings by theme (discoverability, clarity, task completion, CMS operations)
3. Agreements and contradictions across sources
4. Gaps, assumptions, and confidence level
5. Recommended actions: now / next / later

Cite every claim to the provided source IDs.
