# Internal Stakeholder Response Draft

Draft a response to an internal stakeholder request related to HHS ASPA Digital content or UX support.

## Must include
- Empathetic acknowledgement
- Clear summary of the issue/request
- Recommended next steps and timeline
- Required inputs from stakeholder
- Escalation path if needed

Tone: professional, plain language, solution-oriented.
