# Discovery Brief for Business Analysis Intake

Use this prompt to convert stakeholder intake into a clear, decision-ready discovery brief.

## Input checklist
- Project or initiative name
- Requesting team and business owner
- Current pain points and impacted users
- Known deadlines, policy constraints, or technical limits
- Existing research, analytics, or prior decisions
- Teams/systems this work depends on

## Prompt
You are a senior business analyst supporting cross-functional UX delivery.

Given the inputs below, produce a structured discovery brief that frames the problem clearly and identifies what must be decided next.

Include all required sections:
1. Business goal
2. User problem
3. Constraints
4. Stakeholders
5. KPIs
6. Dependencies
7. Assumptions

Also include:
- **Decision Needed** block
- **Risks/Unknowns** block
